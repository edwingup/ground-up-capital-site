Why confidence scoring matters in AI-driven underwriting

By Edwin Sequeira • Feb 2026 • 6 min read

In underwriting, the question isn’t “is AI accurate?” It’s “what can I trust—and what should I review?” Confidence scoring gives a workflow answer.

Confidence converts extraction into a workflow

Without confidence, reviewers re-check everything. With confidence, teams triage and review only what needs attention.

What to score

  • Field-level values (NOI, occupancy, expense lines)
  • Document completeness and missing inputs
  • Consistency checks across sections

Confidence builds stakeholder trust

Confidence plus citations makes verification and explanation straightforward.
