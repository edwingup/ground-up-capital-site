Investing SFR + Small MF

Why SFR and small multifamily still work in today’s market

By Edwin Sequeira • Feb 2026 • 8 min read

When the market gets noisy, operator fundamentals matter more than narratives. SFR and small multifamily can work because you can understand demand, control execution, and keep optionality.

Demand is local

The right neighborhoods keep renting even when headlines shift.

Small assets are operationally “fixable”

  • Preventive maintenance reduces surprises
  • Standardized turns shorten vacancy
  • Simple metrics improve consistency

Optionality matters

Underwrite multiple paths: STR may have mid-term or long-term fallback depending on location and regulation.
