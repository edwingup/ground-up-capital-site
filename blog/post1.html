Investing AI Underwriting

How AI is changing commercial real estate underwriting

By Edwin Sequeira • Feb 2026 • 8 min read

AI is reshaping underwriting—but not by replacing analysts. Near-term value is practical: faster extraction, more consistency, and clearer auditability so humans spend time on judgment.

The biggest time sink isn’t analysis—it’s extraction

Teams lose hours hunting for numbers across OMs, rent rolls, and trailing statements. AI can map unstructured docs into structured fields.

Standardization improves decision velocity

  • Fewer “where is this number?” loops
  • More consistent first-pass screening
  • Better handoffs between sourcing and underwriting

Confidence scoring matters

Real workflows need triage. Confidence scoring routes uncertain fields to review and lets teams move faster on high-confidence items.

Humans still own assumptions

AI accelerates what’s written. Humans decide what’s conservative, what’s risky, and what changes the downside case.

AI-assisted underwriting is changing how operators evaluate deals—faster extraction, fewer manual loops, better clarity on what matters.
