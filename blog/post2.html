Investing Deal Screening

From OM PDFs to structured data: the future of deal screening

By Edwin Sequeira • Feb 2026 • 7 min read

Deal flow is abundant. Attention is scarce. Structured data turns screening into a repeatable pipeline where teams compare deals consistently and escalate the right ones.

Structured data is the missing layer

The missing layer is a “document-to-fields” bridge that standardizes what matters across deals.

What to structure first

  • Core performance: income, expenses, NOI, occupancy
  • Business plan: capex items, timelines
  • Risks: concentration, deferred maintenance

Where AI fits

AI accelerates extraction, but products must add citations, confidence, and review queues to be trustworthy.
