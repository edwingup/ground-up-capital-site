Investing STR vs MTR

Short-term vs mid-term rentals: risk, volatility, and strategy

By Edwin Sequeira • Feb 2026 • 7 min read

STR can produce strong revenue in the right pockets—but it introduces volatility and operational complexity. Mid-term rentals trade upside for stability. Start with risk, not optimism.

Understand the demand source

  • STR: events, tourism, seasonality
  • MTR: traveling professionals, relocations, insurance stays

Operational complexity is real

  • Cleaning and guest communication
  • Furnishings replacement cycles
  • Platform policies and reviews

Always underwrite a fallback

STR → MTR → long-term should pencil. If the fallback doesn’t work, the upside case shouldn’t save the deal.
